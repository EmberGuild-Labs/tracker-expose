How it works

  1. We fetch the page server-side — your IP stays hidden from the target
  2. All scripts, images, iframes, and links are parsed for third-party domains
  3. Each domain is checked against EasyPrivacy and EasyList blocklists
  4. Results are categorized and graded by severity
